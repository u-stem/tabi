## New Features

### Bulk Time Adjustment

When you change a schedule's start or end time, a dialog now asks whether to shift all subsequent schedules by the same amount. Useful when delays occur during a trip.

- Preview of times before and after the change
- Schedules exceeding 24 hours and accommodations are automatically skipped

### Shared Trips on Home Screen

Shared trips have been moved from a dedicated page to a tab on the home screen. Quickly switch between your trips and shared trips.

## Improvements

### Drag & Drop

- You can now reorder items to the end of a list
- When moving a schedule back to candidates, it is now inserted at the drop position
- Unified drop indicator display

### Candidate Panel

- Memo is now displayed on candidate cards
- Added text labels to candidate buttons on mobile

### Other

- Improved visibility of transit names and times
- Improved input form placeholders with specific examples
- Added notes about username and password requirements
