## New Features

### Avatar Settings

You can now choose a profile avatar from the Settings page. Pick from 12 illustration styles and shuffle through candidates to find your favorite.

- Displayed in the header, member lists, friend lists, and real-time presence
- Initials are shown if no avatar is set
- Change or reset anytime from the "Avatar" section in Settings

### Multiple URLs

You can now register up to 5 URLs per schedule or candidate. Keep official sites, maps, reviews, and booking links together.

- Add more URL fields with the "Add URL" button
- All registered URLs are displayed on the card
- Supported in exports and shared link pages

### Transit Search Links

For travel-category schedules with departure and arrival locations, transit search links are now auto-generated.

- Train, bullet train, and bus: Yahoo! Transit
- Car and taxi: Google Maps route search
- Also available on candidate cards and shared link pages

## Improvements

- Improved candidate card display with better category and detail visibility
- Added address, route, and memo icons to shared link page schedules

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
