## New Features

### Schedule Coordination

When your trip dates are undecided, members can now propose and vote on date options.

- Choose "Coordinate schedule" when creating a trip to start in coordination mode
- Add date options by selecting ranges on the calendar
- Each member responds with OK / NG / Undecided for each option
- Finalizing a date automatically sets the trip dates and transitions to normal planning

### Share Link

During schedule coordination, the share link shows date options and response status.

- Generate a link from the share button on the trip detail page
- Share links have an expiration date and can be regenerated

### Managing Date Options

- Add, delete individually, or select and delete multiple date options
- Duplicate date ranges cannot be added
- Duplicating a trip also copies the schedule coordination

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
