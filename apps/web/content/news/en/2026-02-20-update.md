## New Features

### Expense Management

You can now record trip expenses and automatically calculate how much each member owes.

- Add, edit, and delete expenses from the "Expenses" tab in trip details
- Select a payer and target members, then choose equal or custom split
- Total spending, per-member balances, and settlement amounts are calculated automatically

### Settlement Summary

After recording expenses, see at a glance who owes whom and how much.

- Shows per-member surplus or deficit
- Calculates the minimum number of transfers needed for settlement

## Improvements

- Added an option to include expense data in exports
- Unified the design of selection buttons in trip creation and expense dialogs
- Added character counters to text input fields in dialogs
- Members with linked expenses are now blocked from being removed from the trip
