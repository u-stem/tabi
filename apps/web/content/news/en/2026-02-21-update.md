## New Features

### Cover Image

You can now set a cover image for your trips.

- Upload a cover image from the trip create/edit dialog
- Supports JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats (up to 3MB)
- Drag to adjust the image display position
- Displayed on home screen cards and the trip detail header
- Duplicating a trip also copies the cover image

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
