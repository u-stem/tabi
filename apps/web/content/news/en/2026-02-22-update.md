## New Features

### Guest Mode

You can now try the app without creating an account.

- Tap "Try as Guest" on the login or sign-up screen to get started immediately
- Create one trip and experience basic features like adding schedules and spots
- Guest accounts expire after 7 days

### Account Registration

Register an account during guest mode to keep your trip data.

- Tap "Create Account" from the banner at the top of the screen
- Just set a username, display name, and password to complete registration

### Guest Mode Limitations

The following features are available after creating an account:

- Friends
- Bookmarks
- Groups
- Trip share links
- Settings (profile, avatar, password change)

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
