## New Features

### Mobile-Optimized Screen

When accessing from a smartphone browser, the app now automatically switches to a touch-optimized interface.

- Bottom navigation for quick access to main pages
- Home, trips, bookmarks, friends, and settings are all optimized for mobile
- Swipe to switch tabs on the trip detail page

### Display Mode Toggle

Select "Desktop View" from the header menu to switch to the desktop interface at any time.

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
