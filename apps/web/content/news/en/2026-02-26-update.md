## New Features

### Souvenir List

Manage your souvenir shopping list from the "Souvenirs" tab in trip details.

- Record item name, recipient, address, URL, and memo
- Mark items as purchased with a checkbox
- Addresses are displayed as map search links
- Bulk delete multiple items
- Your souvenir list is private and not shared with other members

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
