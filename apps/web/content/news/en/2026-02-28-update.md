## Improvements

### Souvenir Priority

A priority button has been added to the souvenir add/edit form.

- **Must Buy** (red): Items you definitely want to purchase
- **If Possible** (yellow): Items to buy if there's time
- No priority set: Default

### Sort by Priority

A sort button has been added to the souvenir tab toolbar. Sort by "Must Buy" > "If Possible" > unset, or switch back to creation order.

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
