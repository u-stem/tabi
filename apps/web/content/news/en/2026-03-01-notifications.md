## New Features

### In-App Notifications

Check notifications from the bell icon in the header. The following events trigger notifications:

- Member invitation, removal, or role change
- Schedule added, updated, or deleted
- Schedule voting started or ended
- Expense added

A badge with the unread count is shown on the bell icon when there are unread notifications.

### Push Notifications

Enable browser push notifications to receive trip updates even when the app is not open. Enable push notifications from the "Notifications" tab in Settings.

### Notification Settings

In the "Notifications" tab in Settings, toggle in-app and push notifications on or off for each event type.

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
