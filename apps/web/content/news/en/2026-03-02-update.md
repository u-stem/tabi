## New Features

### Weather and Temperature

You can now record weather and temperature for each trip day.

- **Weather**: Choose from 11 types including sunny, cloudy, rainy, and snowy
- **Two-stage weather**: Record weather changes like "Sunny then Cloudy" by tapping the second icon
- **Temperature**: Enter high and low temperatures in °C

### Day Tab Display

When weather is set, icons are displayed directly on the day tabs (Day 1, Day 2, etc.) for an at-a-glance overview of the trip's weather.

### How to Edit

Tap the weather area in the day details to start editing. On mobile, a selection panel slides up from the bottom.
