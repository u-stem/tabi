## New Features

### Password Reset

Users with a registered email address can now reset their password from "Forgot Password" on the login screen.

A confirmation link will be sent to your registered email. Click the link to set a new password.

### Email Registration

Register or change your email address from the Settings page. A confirmation email will be sent — click the link to complete setup.

Your email address is only used for password resets.

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
