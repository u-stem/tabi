## New Features

### Quick Poll

A voting feature for group decisions like choosing a destination or restaurant. Create polls from "Quick Poll" in the side menu.

### Share via Link

After creating a poll, a share link is generated. Anyone with the link can vote without logging in. Easily share via LINE or messaging apps.

### Options

Customize your poll with these settings:

- **Allow multiple selections** — Each person can vote for multiple options
- **Show results before voting** — View current results before casting a vote (when off, results are shown after voting)

### Poll Management

Manage all your polls from the "Quick Poll" page. Close voting or delete polls from here.
