## New Features

### Roulette

A random selection tool for when you can't decide on a destination or activity. Access it from the "Tools" section on your profile page.

### Three Modes

- **Prefecture** — Randomly picks one of Japan's 47 prefectures. Filter by region (Hokkaido/Tohoku, Kanto, etc.)
- **Custom** — Enter your own options and spin. Use it for restaurants, activities, or anything else
- **Bookmark** — Randomly picks from a saved bookmark list

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
