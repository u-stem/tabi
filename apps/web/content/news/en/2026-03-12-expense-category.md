## New Features

### Expense Categories

You can now assign a category when adding or editing expenses. Choose from 8 categories:

- Transportation
- Accommodation
- Food & Drink
- Sightseeing & Entertainment
- Shopping
- Communication
- Insurance & Fees
- Other

### Category Totals

The settlement panel now shows total amounts and counts by category, giving you a clear breakdown of trip expenses.

### Export Improvements

- Added a category column to the expense sheet
- Per-member cost breakdowns are now included as columns

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
