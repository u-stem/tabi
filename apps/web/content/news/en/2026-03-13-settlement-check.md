## New Features

### Settlement Check

In the settlement section of the Expenses tab, you can now check off each settlement item to record whether payment has been completed.

- Only the payer and recipient can toggle the check
- Check status is reflected in real time and the other party is notified
- When expenses change, related checks are automatically reset

### Unsettled Summary

- **Profile page**: View a list of unsettled trips and amounts
- **Home screen**: A badge is shown on trips with outstanding settlements

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
