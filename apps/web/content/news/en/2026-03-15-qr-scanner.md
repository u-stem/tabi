## New Features

### QR Code Scanner

A "Scan QR" button has been added to the Friends page. Scan friend request QR codes directly within the app.

- **Camera scan**: Scan QR codes in real time using the camera (mobile)
- **Read from image**: Read QR codes from screenshots or saved images (mobile & desktop)

No need to launch a separate camera app — add friends smoothly from within the app.

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
