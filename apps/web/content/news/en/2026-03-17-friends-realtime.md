## New Features

### Reactions

When viewing a trip page at the same time as other members, a reaction button appears. Tap it to send an emoji reaction.

- Tap the reaction button and select an emoji
- Reactions float up on every member's screen who has the trip open
- Consecutive sends are limited by a 1-second cooldown

Available on both desktop and mobile.

### Real-Time Friend Sync

Friend-related actions are now reflected in real time on the other person's Friends page.

- Sending a friend request
- Accepting or declining a request
- Canceling a sent request
- Removing a friend

Changes are reflected automatically without reloading the page.

### Cancel Sent Requests

You can now cancel pending friend requests from the "Sent Requests" section on the Friends page. Canceling immediately removes the request from the recipient's list.

### Souvenir Sharing

Turn on "Share with members" when adding or editing a souvenir item to make it visible to other trip members.

By default, purchase status is also visible to members. Choose a sharing style to change this:

- **Recommendation**: Information sharing only. Purchase status is hidden from members
- **Errand**: Purchase status is visible to members (same as default)

Additional details:

- Shared items show the poster's name
- Other members' items are view-only — they cannot be edited or deleted
- A share icon is shown on your own shared items
- Items remain private unless explicitly shared

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
