## New Features

### English Language Support

The entire app can now be displayed in English.

- Switch between Japanese and English using the language toggle in the header menu
- Your language preference is saved in the browser and remembered for future visits
- If no preference is set, the language is automatically selected based on your browser settings

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
