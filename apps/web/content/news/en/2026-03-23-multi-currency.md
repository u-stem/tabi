## New Features

### Multi-Currency Support

Managing expenses on international trips is now easier. You can record expenses in 12 different currencies.

- Supported currencies: JPY, USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, CAD, CHF, CNY, HKD, KRW, SGD, TWD
- Set a base currency when creating a trip. Settlements are calculated in the base currency
- When adding an expense in a different currency, exchange rates are auto-filled based on ECB (European Central Bank) data
- Exchange rates can be manually adjusted to match your actual exchange rate
- The expense list shows converted amounts alongside the original for expenses in non-base currencies

Please send us your feedback from the Settings page.
