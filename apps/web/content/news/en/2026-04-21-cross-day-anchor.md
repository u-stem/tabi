## New Features

### Pin schedules around entries that carry over from the previous day

You can now drag a schedule onto an entry that continues from the previous day — for example, a hotel checkout — and pin it immediately before or after.

- Drop on the upper half of the card → pin the schedule **before** it
- Drop on the lower half of the card → pin the schedule **after** it
- The pinned position is preserved even if you later change the schedule's start time
- The "Sort by time" button clears all manual pins and reorders everything by time again

Example: on a day with breakfast at 08:00, you may want a sightseeing stop placed right after the checkout. Previously the time-based ordering could place the stop before the checkout. With this change, dropping the stop onto the lower half of the checkout keeps the intended order.
